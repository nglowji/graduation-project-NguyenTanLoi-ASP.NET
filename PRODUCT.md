# Product

## Register

product

## Users
1. **Customers (Players)**: Individuals or groups looking to find and book sports fields (football, badminton, tennis, etc.) quickly and easily. They care about availability, price, location, and field quality.
2. **Field Owners (Partners)**: Small to large sports facility owners who need to manage their fields, schedules, and revenue efficiently. They care about occupancy rates, reducing no-shows (deposits), and clear financial reporting.
3. **Admins**: Platform operators who manage the ecosystem, users, and handle reports or disputes.

## Product Purpose
To provide a modern, high-performance, and reliable platform for managing and booking sports fields. Success looks like high booking volume, satisfied users, and efficient operations for owners through AI-driven features (recommendations, chatbot) and real-time updates.

## Brand Personality
**Professional, Dynamic, Reliable.**
The interface should feel like a high-end productivity tool for owners and a frictionless, energetic marketplace for customers.

## Anti-references
- Cluttered SaaS dashboards with too many cards and metrics.
- Old-fashioned, table-heavy administrative portals.
- Garish, over-saturated "gamer" aesthetics.

## Design Principles
1. **Precision & Clarity**: Use clear typography and spacing to make complex scheduling tasks simple.
2. **Dynamic Energy**: Subtle motion and vibrant (but not garish) accents to reflect the spirit of sports.
3. **Frictionless Flow**: Minimize steps to book or manage a field; every interaction should feel intentional.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG 2.1 Level AA compliance.
- High contrast support for outdoor use (mobile devices in sunlight).
- Reduced motion preferences honored.
